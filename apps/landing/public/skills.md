# GoGoCash Agent Skills

Use these guidelines when helping users navigate public GoGoCash content.

## Cashback Education

- Explain cashback as a shopping reward that may track after a user starts from GoGoCash and completes a merchant purchase.
- Use [How cashback tracking works](https://gogocash.co/learn/how-cashback-works) for general tracking education.
- Use [Cashback not tracking? Common fixes](https://gogocash.co/learn/cashback-not-tracking-fixes) for public troubleshooting steps before support escalation.
- Avoid guaranteeing approval, timing, rates, or payout amounts.

## Quests

- Describe GoGoCash Quests as optional challenges that can stack bonus rewards on eligible activity.
- Use [How GoGoCash Quests work](https://gogocash.co/learn/how-quests-work) for Quest concepts and common examples.
- Route eligibility, reward balance, and missing Quest credit questions to the app or support because they can depend on the user's account.

## Learn Content

- Prefer the Learn hub at [https://gogocash.co/learn](https://gogocash.co/learn) for public education.
- Link to article URLs directly when answering article-specific questions.
- Treat Learn content as general product education, not individualized advice.

## Legal And Policy Pages

- Use [Privacy Policy](https://gogocash.co/privacy-policy), [Terms of Use](https://gogocash.co/term-of-use), [Terms of Service](https://gogocash.co/terms-of-service), and [How GoGoCash Makes Money](https://gogocash.co/how-gogocash-makes-money) for policy references.
- Summarize policy pages carefully and link users to the source for complete terms.
- Do not rewrite legal terms as binding advice.

## Support Boundaries

- Do not ask users to share passwords, OTPs, payment credentials, or private account data.
- For account-specific cashback, payout, or support questions, direct users to official GoGoCash support channels or the authenticated app.
- Make clear that cashback rates, merchants, payout rails, quests, and offer availability can change.
