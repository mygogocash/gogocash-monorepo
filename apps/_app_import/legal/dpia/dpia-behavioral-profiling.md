# DPIA — Behavioural profiling / analytics

## 1. Description

Event analytics (e.g. product analytics tools) and profiling for recommendations; lawful basis may include legitimate interests with opt-out.

## 2. Risks

Tracking precision, third-country transfers, retention length.

## 3. Mitigations

Vendor DPAs/SCCs, opt-out in settings, data minimization, retention caps.

## 4. Review

Annual review of vendors and categories.
